# AI In Action

A hands-on AI tech community built on the philosophy of **"Learn AI, Use AI, Build Projects."**

We believe the best way to learn AI is not by watching tutorials — it's by building real projects. Through our online platform ([aiinaction.top](https://aiinaction.top)) and offline events, we help everyone from beginners to expert developers master practical AI skills.

## Our Vision

To become the most practical AI tech community, empowering everyone to solve real-world problems with AI.

## Our Mission

Through a dual-engine model of **"online learning + offline practice,"** we are building an open, co-creative, and shared AI ecosystem — accelerating individual growth and driving industry innovation.
